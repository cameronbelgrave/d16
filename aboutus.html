THE DOBBS 16, Directed by Jennifer Carnevale

The Dobbs 16 is a coed a cappella choir from The Masters School in Dobbs Ferry NY. Members hail from all over the world, and come together to share their love of music. This select choir offers each member an opportunity to nurture ensemble skills as well as build solid musicianship. The group has travelled abroad to Japan and China, performed the National Anthem at a Phillies game and a Red Bulls Soccer game, performed on the Tyra Banks Show with the cast of Glee, and competed in the International Championship of HIgh School A Cappella. The Dobbs 16 has recorded 5 CDs: This Is How I Feel Today (www.JohnAlec.com), Late For Practice (Avatar Studios, NYC), Lost in Shanghai (Avatar Studios, NYC), I’ll Be There (www.JohnAlec.com), and Takin’ it to the Streets (Quad Studio, NYC).
